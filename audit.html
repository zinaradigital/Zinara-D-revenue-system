Frequently Asked Questions

What if my problem isn't infrastructure-related?

We'll tell you. The audit identifies whether your revenue failure is structural (fixable with systems) or not. If it's not infrastructure, we won't recommend our services.

Do I need to prepare anything for the audit?

Having approximate numbers helps: monthly inbound volume, estimated response times, current conversion rate (if known). But if you don't have these, we'll work with what you have.

What happens if I don't want to proceed after the audit?

Nothing. The audit is diagnostic. There's no obligation to proceed with implementation. Many businesses use the audit insights to fix issues internally.

How detailed is the Revenue Loss Map?

Very detailed. You receive specific failure points, quantified leakage (based on your data), and prioritized repair recommendations with estimated ROI for each fix.

Can the audit be done remotely?

Yes. Most audits are conducted remotely via video call. On-site audits are available for businesses in Nairobi and surrounding areas if needed.
