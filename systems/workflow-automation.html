Diagnostic Checklist — Is Manual Workflow Killing Revenue?

  • Tasks require constant manual follow-up
  • Operations depend on specific people being available
  • Data moves manually between systems
  • Task completion is inconsistent
  • Scale increases chaos rather than revenue
  • Team spends time on repetitive work
  • Follow-ups get forgotten
  • Process execution varies by person

If 4+ items checked: Workflow automation is critical.

Example Automation: Lead Follow-Up System

Manual Process (Before)

  1. Lead fills website form
  2. Email notification received
  3. Someone manually copies to spreadsheet
  4. Someone manually sends first message
  5. Someone manually sets reminder to follow up
  6. Follow-up happens if remembered
  7. No visibility on completion

Automated Process (After)

  1. Lead fills website form
  2. Automatically captured in CRM
  3. Instant automated acknowledgment sent
  4. Qualification questions sent automatically
  5. Follow-up sequence triggered based on responses
  6. Human alerted only when intervention needed
  7. Complete visibility and tracking
